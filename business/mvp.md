# MVP (Minimum Vaiable Product) in Software

> ## Freemium vs Premium

- Iteration Cycle is a vital part of any business i.e.,
  - observe & be prepared to make changes.

## Iteration Cycle has `6 steps`:

- Watch to see what works
- Define what could be improved
  and always know what are your options to do this
- Weigh up your options based on experiences
- Decide which one would have an impact
- Make the changes
- Assess the change was it `+ve and -ve`
